How I Train

My Training Philosophy

Balancing school, finance clubs, and a TA role means my training has to be structured. Here's how I organize fitness across my week:

  1. Strength & Conditioning (3x / week)
    • Monday: Upper body push — chest, shoulders, triceps
    • Wednesday: Upper body pull — back, biceps, rear delts
    • Friday: Lower body — squat focus, hamstrings, calves
    • Progressive overload every session, tracking PRs in a log
  2. Cardio & Sport (2–3x / week)
    • Tuesday morning: 4–6 mile run around campus or trail
    • Thursday: Pickup basketball or interval sprints (45 min)
    • Weekend: Hike, flag football, or recreational basketball
    • Goal: Keep overall endurance up for basketball season
  3. Recovery & Nutrition
    • Daily: 10 minutes of dynamic stretching post-workout
    • Sleep: 7.5–8 hours minimum on school nights
    • Nutrition: High protein focus, whole foods — and yes, I grill my own meals
    • Weekly: Full foam rolling and mobility session
  4. Competitive & Fan Habits
    • BYU Cougars games — in the student section whenever possible
    • Follow Raiders, Warriors, and A's game-by-game
    • Intramural basketball — always on a team at BYU rec
    • Hiking a new trail in Utah every semester
